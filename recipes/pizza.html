Pizza Buffalina

Description

Follow along as we make a traditional pizza buffalina. Whether you choose to make your dough from scratch or not, we will use fresh ingredients such as fresh cherry tomatoes and mozzarella di buffola.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (fresh or store-bought)
  • Pizza Tomato Sauce (fresh or store-bought
  • Mozzarella di buffola
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmiggiano
  • Fresh Basil (optional)

Recipe

  1. Pre-heat your oven at 220C degrees
  2. Lay the dough in an oven proof tray
  3. Using a fork, make small holes in the dough
  4. Generously glaze the dough with olive oil
  5. Sprinkle some grated parmiggiano on top of the dough
  6. Spread a spoon full of tomato sauce on the dough
  7. Cut or pull apart the mozzarella and place it evenly across the dough
  8. Slice some cherry tomatoes and spread evenly across the dough
  9. Place the pizza in the tray in the oven, cook for 18 to 20 minutes, or until golden
  10. Once the pizza is cooked, add the final touches of fresh basil and olive oil
  11. Bon Appetit!

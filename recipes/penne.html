Penne Broccoli

Description

Try our recipe for penne with broccoli and salmon for some of us and this acidic sauce that really brings the dish together.

Ingredients

  • Penne Rigate
  • A Broccoli
  • Salmon (optional)
  • Soya Sauce
  • Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Recipe

  1. Prepare the broccoli by cutting the fleurettes individually
  2. Blanche the broccoli
  3. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil
  4. Add the pasta and cook for as long as recommended
  5. At this stage, prepare the salmon strips, if you're using it in the recipe
  6. Once the pasta is cooked, remove from water and place back in the pot with broccoli and salmon, off the heat
  7. Add the olive oil, lemon juice, soya sauce and pepper to taste
  8. Lightly stir to mix the sauce in, without breaking the fleurettes and the salmon
  9. Serve Hot!
